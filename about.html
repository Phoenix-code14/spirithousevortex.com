About Us

Spirithouse Vortex Records is an underground record
label that has set its sights on becoming a pioneer in the vibrant
and diverse world of electronic music. With a dedicated focus on
Amapiano, Deep House, Afro Tech, and Afro House, we are
committed to bringing forth a new wave of sounds that embody
the rich cultural heritage and innovative spirit of South Africa.

Founded in the heart of Burgersfort in Praktiseer, South Africa,
Spirithouse Vortex Records serves as a testament to the boundless creativity
and unyielding passion of the South African music scene
. Our label is proudly owned by Thobejane Thabiso, also known as
Phoenix SA - a dynamic and visionary presence
in the industry.

At Spirithouse Vortex Records, we embrace the notion of collaboration
and the celebration of emerging talent. We stand as a beacon
of opportunity for aspiring artists, nurturing their creativity and
providing a platform for their unique voices to be heard. We are
driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a deep-rooted
belief in the transformative power of music.

Embracing the ethos of innovation and cultural fusion,
Spirithouse Vortex Records invites you to join us on an exhilarating
journey that transcends boundaries and captures the spirit of a new era in
electronic music.

We are Spirithouse Vortex Records. Welcome
to our sonic universe.
